These artists have not only shaped my musical taste but have also influenced my perspectives and emotions in profound ways. My top 3

Music creates a soundtrack for our lives, and three artists have particularly resonated with me, shaping my tastes and influencing my outlook. These artists are storytellers with unique styles, passion, and creativity.

Taylor Swift

From country prodigy to global pop sensation, Taylor's storytelling, lyrical prowess, and versatile music have provided comfort, joy, and empowerment

The Weeknd

His distinctive voice and genre-blending music weave dark, emotional narratives, offering both solace and excitement with deep and complex sounds.

Blackpink

Blackpink-Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa, they seamlessly blend hip-hop, pop, and EDM, captivating a massive international fanbase with their dynamic stage presence and music.

These artists—Taylor Swift, The Weeknd, and BLACKPINK—have shaped my musical journey with their unique styles and passion. Taylor Swift's evolution from country to pop, The Weeknd's genre-blending R&B, and BLACKPINK's powerful presence in K-pop have all inspired and enriched my life.
